# The Ultimate Coffee Guide for Developers

Best coffee types for different coding moods:
- Deep Work / Debugging → Ethiopian Yirgacheffe (bright, floral)
- All-night Coding → Colombian Supremo (balanced, chocolate notes)
- Creative Brainstorming → Kenyan AA (bold, berry-like)

Pro tip: Never drink espresso after 4 PM if you want to sleep before 2 AM.
